Overview

Fort De Soto Park in St. Petersburg offers one of the state’s most popular off-leash dog beaches. Dogs can enjoy sand, surf, and designated fenced areas.

Rules

  • Dogs must be leashed outside designated beach/park areas
  • Always clean up after your pet
  • Check park signs for seasonal restrictions

Amenities

Fenced play areas, rinse stations, shaded spots, and convenient parking.
